# Raw research extract — Endor Labs pricing

Captured: 2026-07-16
Source: https://www.endorlabs.com/pricing

- Free Developer/AURI tier: local scanning and read-only vulnerability intelligence; no UI, policy, or scan history.
- Core and Pro: paid, per-contributor, quote-led; deeper scanning, policies, integrations, reporting, and workflow features.
- Product SKUs at capture: Code, Open Source, Agent Governance, Package Firewall, Patches, and SBOM Hub.
- Cloud marketplace and on-premises/Outpost options.
- A separate official demo page stated pricing begins around $10,000/year; current pricing page is authoritative and does not publish Core/Pro amounts.
