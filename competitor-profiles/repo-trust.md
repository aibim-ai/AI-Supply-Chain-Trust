## Competitive implication

This is the closest scoring-method competitor and is stronger on fake-star analysis, real-world adoption, maintainer concentration, local privacy, and CSV output. AI Supply Chain Trust is stronger on zero-install hosted public contexts, public REST/MCP, historical fixes/CVEs, regression leads, progressive enrichment, and AI/MCP/model risk pillars. Do not claim uniqueness in explainable or deterministic trust scoring.
